Week 2 Recap:

This 2nd week proved to be a tough week for top tier running backs. Usual top prospects LeSean McCoy and Ezekiel Elliot both put up single-digit rushing yards. On a brighter note, Kareem Hunt and Tom Brady both had great weeks.

Winners:

  1. Tom Brady
  2. Kareem Hunt
  3. Michael Crabtree

Losers:

  1. Jordan Howard
    • Listed questionable with shoulder injury
    • Scored .7 Fanasy Points
  2. Ezekiel Elliot
    • Held to single digit yards
  3. Cam Newton
    • Still recovering from offseason surgery

Injury Report:

Week 2 proved to be another tough week for injuries, particularly at the tight end position. Greg Olson, Marshal Yanda, and Garett Bolles are among some names on the injury list for the week.
